# 📊 E-Commerce Performance Insights Dashboard (Task 4)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Performance Insights Dashboard** designed to translate raw transactional data into actionable business strategy. The dashboard visualizes sales velocity, audience acquisition metrics, and logistical distribution patterns.

---

## 📈 Executive Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Based on the dashboard summary, the business achieved the following macroscopic metrics:
* **Total Sales Revenue:** $1.26M
* **Total Units Sold:** 3,535 items
* **Fulfillment Rate:** 19.25% *(Indicates a critical volume of unfulfilled, cancelled, or returned items requiring operational review).*

---

## 🔍 Core Dashboard Insights

### 1. Product Demand Portfolio
* **Top Performers:** **Chairs** ($196K) and **Printers** ($196K) lead gross sales revenue.
* **Trailing Segment:** **Phones** generated the least overall value at $152K.

### 2. Payment Method Distribution
* Consumer transaction preference remains highly balanced. **Credit Cards**, **Online Payments**, and **Cash** payments hold equivalent weight blocks at approximately $0.26M each.

### 3. Marketing & Audience Acquisition
* **Instagram** stands out as the primary conversion pipeline, responsible for **21.78% (770 orders)** of customer acquisition traffic, closely followed by Email marketing at 21.1%.

### 4. Sales Trends & Seasonality
* Multi-year monthly tracking shows severe recurring spikes in mid-year windows, with **June** and **May** repeatedly generating peak transactional volume before tapering off in late summer.
